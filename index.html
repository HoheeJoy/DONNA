I Fell Inlove

Donna

I spend my whole life imagining someone to stay with me.

I always look at my window trying to mesmerize the possibility.

And suddenly God bless me and says "I'll give her to you, just wait patiently."

That motivates me so I ask "I can wait my Lord." and smile dearly.

The first time I saw your face, my eyes lingers on you.

I fell in love immediately and I don't have any clue.

Is this even true? This girl is so adorable I wanna be her boo.

As I touch your hands, that's the time I said "Lord, thank you."

Joy
